Dutch Deal Desk

An LBO returns & financial due-diligence dashboard for Netherlands mid-market private equity. Type a deal in — entry multiple, leverage, exit — and get a full leveraged-buyout model, a value-creation bridge, gross-vs-net fund returns, a DCF cross-check, sensitivity tables, and a diligence scorecard. Tuned to Dutch 2026 tax law, including the earnings-stripping rule (Art. 15b CITA) that off-the-shelf templates ignore.

NL Grid Congestion × Solar Curtailment

Connects Dutch grid-congestion status to the cost of residential solar curtailment, framed around the NL grid rules taking effect 1 July 2026. A spatial layer classifies every area by feed-in congestion from the ACM register; a temporal layer models hourly rooftop-PV generation (pvlib + PVGIS) to compute lost kWh and euro loss. Ships as an interactive choropleth map plus a Streamlit dashboard.

CSRD / ESRS Compliance — Master Thesis

The compliance-scoring component of my MSc thesis: do AI-assisted ESG reporting tools improve ESRS compliance? 38 EU companies (19 AI-platform users vs 19 matched peers) scored against the 12 ESRS standards on a 0–3 rubric, then statistically re-analysed. Finding: the AI group scores marginally higher, but the gap is not statistically significant — a careful, honest null result with full power analysis.

RAAP! Fundraising Benchmark

An income-source benchmark of 14 NGOs in regenerative agriculture and land restoration, built to guide fundraising strategy for RAAP!. An Excel → SQLite pipeline with 13 analytical SQL queries surfaces the key insight: business income (corporates + foundations) is 70% of pooled income and the path to scale, while smaller NGOs anchor on community engagement.
