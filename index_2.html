What I do

Three lines of work, one method: measure first.

Carbon & emissions accounting

analyse

Markets & material flows

Carbon-credit market analysis (Verra, Gold Standard) by project type and price history, plus district-level Material Flow Analysis of agri-waste and biomass supply chains in Python.

advise

ESRS, GRI, and BRSR disclosure support, ESG workshops and executive briefings, and funding strategies for public-benefit organisations grounded in scraped financial data and interviews.
